Banana Pina Colada Smoothie

Description

It's summertime! I don't know about you but I love and I mean LOVE me a Banana Smoothie! The nice cool freshness of bananas with the pleasing texture of a smoothie. I am drooling just thinking about it! One day, I came up with the idea to combine my two favorite drinks into this masterpiece of a smoothie! Hence, the Banana Pina Colada Smoothie was born! If you want to know how to make this heavenly goodness, stick with me and read on!

Ingredients

  • 1 large banana
  • 1 cup coconut juice blend, or more to taste
  • 2 pineapple spears
  • 3 cubes ice
  • 1 tablespoon agave nectar

Steps

  1. Blend banana, coconut juice blend, pineapple, ice, and agave nectar together in a blender until smooth.
